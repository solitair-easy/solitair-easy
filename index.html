Solitaire Easy, also known as Klondike Solitaire Easy, is a simplified version of the classic single-player card game. It provides a straightforward and accessible gameplay experience for beginners and those seeking a more relaxed version of Solitaire.

With a simplified setup, players begin with seven piles of cards, gradually increasing in size. The game allows for moving cards between piles and the tableau by placing a card of the opposite color and one rank lower on top of a face-up card. This straightforward rule makes it easier to form descending sequences of alternating colors on the tableau.

Solitaire Easy also introduces the use of the stock and waste piles, which can aid in strategizing and moving cards around. The stock pile represents the remaining deck of cards, while the waste pile holds the face-up cards that can be utilized.

The game's simplified nature and reduced difficulty level make it an ideal choice for casual players, those new to Solitaire, or anyone seeking a more relaxed gaming experience. Solitaire Easy offers a way to enjoy the timeless appeal of Solitaire in a simplified and accessible format.

Whether you're a seasoned Solitaire player looking for a more laid-back game or a beginner eager to explore the world of card games, Solitaire Easy is a delightful option that brings the joy of Solitaire in an easy-to-grasp and enjoyable package.

In Solitaire Easy, the objective remains the same as in traditional Solitaire: to build four foundation piles, one for each suit, in ascending order from Ace to King. However, this version reduces the complexity and challenging elements found in other variations.

Game controls –

The game controls for Solitaire (Klondike Solitaire) can vary depending on the platform or software you're using to play the game. However, here are some common controls you might encounter:

1. Mouse Controls:

  1. Click and drag: Use the mouse to click on a card and drag it to the desired location, such as moving it to another pile or placing it in a foundation pile.
  2. Double-click: Double-clicking on a card automatically moves it to the appropriate foundation pile if it is eligible.
  3. Clicking on the stock pile: Clicking on the stock pile reveals the next card in the waste pile or deals a new card to the waste pile.

2. Keyboard Controls:

  1. Arrow keys: Use the arrow keys to navigate between the cards on the tableau or between the piles.
  2. Enter or Spacebar: Pressing Enter or the Spacebar can be used to select and move a card or perform an action, such as flipping a card or moving it to a foundation pile.

These controls provide a basic overview, but it's important to note that specific game implementations may have additional or different controls. It's best to refer to the instructions or help section within the game you're playing for precise information on the controls.
